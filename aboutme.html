About Me

My name is Jundi Prima and I have always been interested in learning and exploring new ideas. I completed my primary and secondary education at SMKN 1 Cibinong, a vocational high school in West Java, Indonesia, where I studied automation control and developed a passion for critical thinking and problem-solving.

After completing high school, I pursued a degree in instrumentation control industries at Politeknik Negeri Jakarta, one of the leading polytechnic universities in Indonesia. During my studies, I developed a strong foundation in the principles and applications of instrumentation control industries, which helped me to better understand how to design and control various industrial processes.

Currently, I am exploring the field of programming and its potential applications in solving real-world problems. I am excited to continue learning and growing in this field, and to use my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact on the world around me.
